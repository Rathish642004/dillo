## Quick reference

- **Brand mark:** `assets/logo-dillo.jpg` — red rectangular banner with white "DILLO", navy serif tagline.
- **Two-colour palette:** Red `#D7011F` (primary CTA, eyebrows, signature 4-8px rule), Navy `#0F1F4D` (default text, dark bands).
- **Signature motifs:** the 64×4px red rule under every section header; the squared red "BANNER" badge; uppercase tracked eyebrows above titles; bold display sans paired with italic serif quote.
- **Don't:** gradients beyond hero scrims, hand-drawn SVG illustration, emoji in product copy (only as functional row labels on Contact), pill-shaped CTAs, rounded-card-with-coloured-left-border slop.
- **Voice:** confident, factual, present-tense. "We stitch.", "We deliver." Not "We will…", not "Let's revolutionise…".
